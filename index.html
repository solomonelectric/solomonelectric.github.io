What We Do

Electrical contracting with a design-first mindset.

A good electrical project is not just about pulling wire. It is about understanding the space, planning the system, solving problems before they become expensive, and delivering work that is safe, clean, and built to last.

Service Upgrades

Meter bases, panels, grounding, risers, feeders, and electrical capacity upgrades for modern homes and buildings.

Panels & Circuits

Panel replacements, subpanels, breaker corrections, dedicated circuits, and safer electrical organization.

Remodeling & Rewiring

Kitchen, bath, basement, addition, and whole-home wiring updates planned around the finished space.

Lighting Design & Installation

Recessed lighting, exterior lighting, pendants, switching, controls, layout improvements, and clean installation.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Practical diagnosis for outlets, switches, breakers, lighting, damaged wiring, and electrical issues that need a real answer.

Design-Build Electrical

Early planning support for homeowners, builders, and remodelers who want electrical decisions made before the walls are closed.
